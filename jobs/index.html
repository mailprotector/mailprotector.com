Why Mailprotector?

At Mailprotector our goal is to empower our channel partners with the very best email services in the business. We put 100% into everything we do, and strongly believe that every member of the Mailprotector team has the potential to bring about big changes and grow the organization in powerful ways.

We like to solve big problems in creative ways, and are always looking for ways to challenge ourselves to be better. If you want to work in a fun, but fast paced environment where everyone involved takes personal ownership and pride in the products and services we offer, then you'll probably love working here.

Location, location, location...

Mailprotector HQ is located in the prospering downtown micro-metropolis of Greenville, SC. It's called "Green"ville for a reason. Step out the front door and stroll along the lush, tree-lined Main St., and in a few minutes you'll find yourself standing on Liberty Bridge viewing the stunning Falls Park (lunch breaks are pretty great here). The Upstate SC area is vibrant, authentic, and full of variety, and provides the perfect setting to enjoy the optimal work/life balance. Plus we're just a stone's throw from the mountains and the beach. It's an amazing city, and we highly recomment that you pay Greenville a visit even if you have no interest in working here. You'll be happy you did.
