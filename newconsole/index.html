Make THE
Switch 10 Compelling Reasons to Migrate
to the New Mailprotector Console
01

It's blazing fast!

Get your team, and your clients in and out of the console or quarantine fast. We've optimized every little interaction, so you'll see dramatically improved speed across the site.

02

Provisioning is a breeze.

Adding a new domain is quick and painless with User Sync that supports Active Directory, Office 365, and Google's G Suite sources.

03

Message Reviews blow the old Email Security Reports away.

Direct sign in (with inbox authencitation similar to Bracket) securely takes users to their message review without the need to remember their username and password.

Email can be safely previewed from in the console before releasing, while detailed filtering analytics and delivery timeline let you see the big picture to determine if a sender is trustworthy or not. It looks great on mobile too!

04

Get precision spam control.

Adjust how promotional emails, SPF failures, and spoofed addresses are scored. The new console gives you the power to fine-tune filtering settings to accomodate unique needs with ease.

05

It's way easier to get around.

We've totally rethought the navigation so everything is more intuitive. There's also an all-new Finder designed to get you to the right place quickly with options to favorite your most common resources.

06

Notifications keep you in the know.

Our systems will keep an eye on things and let you know right away if there's something you should be alerted to. Managers can even control when and and how they receive their notifications.

07

Message Reviews aren't limited to 2 per day.

On the old console Email Summary Reports were limited to being sent twice daily. But now users can quickly and easily customize how often they want to receive Message Review notifications at the bottom of every review.

08

Better manager delegation.

We listened to your feedback and made it a lot easier to create and modify roles for managers. You can even choose which default role to start with when a manager signs in.

09

Turbo-powered message rules.

We've expanded message rule criteria and actions, allowing for greater compliance control. You also now have the ability to create extremely powerful rules using regular expressions.

10

See domain status at a glance.

When you visit the overview page for a domain, the UI lets you know if there are issues with mail flow, MX records, SPF records, or autodiscover with expanded details just a click away.
